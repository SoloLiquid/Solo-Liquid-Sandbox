## // THE SANDBOX PROTOCOL: HOW TO JOIN

### 01 / DOWNLOAD ASSETS
All official files (logos, audio, visual, video) are in the `/assets` folder. 
You can download them and use them freely for digital projects.

### 02 / UPLOAD YOUR RECREATE ASSETS
*Important: This is ONLY for sharing your own fan-made/recreated assets with the community. (This is NOT for website bounty submissions).*

To share your assets with other creators, follow these steps:
1. **Fork** this repository to your own GitHub account.
2. Upload your files into the `/recreate` folder. (Please make a new folder with your name, like `/recreate/your-name/`).
3. Click "New Pull Request" to submit your assets to our `main` branch.

### 03 / APPROVAL & COMMUNITY SHARING
* We will check every Pull Request before it goes live.
* **No files will be added to the main project without our review.**
* Once approved, your recreated assets will be merged into the `/recreate` folder for everyone in the community to use, remix, and share.
